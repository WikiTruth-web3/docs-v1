### Future Outlook

Evidence Market's vision is to build a Web3 on-chain crime and truth-exposing platform based on privacy encryption technology: Technology is responsible for being "verifiable", the economic model is responsible for being "sustainable", and the community is responsible for being "governable", making the defense of justice no longer a lonely fight for a few.

#### Technology Development Direction

- **AI Integration**: Exploring the use of AI for non-intrusive feature analysis and classification of encrypted evidence to improve market efficiency and authenticity.
- **Cross-Chain Interoperability**: Deploying across chains via protocols like LayerZero to allow more public chain users to participate.
- **Privacy Computing**: Introducing technologies like MPC / FHE to achieve more complex, protected data collaboration.

#### Frontier Technology Exploration

- **Zero-Knowledge Proofs (ZKP)**: Researching how to prove evidence formats/keywords without leaking content to enhance credibility.
- **Private Transactions**: Further integrating ZKP/MPC/FHE on top of Sapphire to achieve finer-grained permission control and verifiable privacy custody.

#### Ecosystem Vision

In the future, Evidence Market will cover the full-chain services of evidence collection, verification, trading, disclosure, and legal aid. It will also cooperate with global legal institutions, media organizations, and public welfare groups to enhance the accessibility and executability of the truth.

#### Milestones and Delivery Plan

1. **Short-Term (Testnet Phase)**: Complete privacy token EIP-712 testing, parameter tuning, public security testing, and Bug Bounty.
2. **Medium-Term (Early Mainnet)**: Release mainnet contracts and address lists, launch DAO parameter governance and emergency funds, and release multi-language documentation and SDKs.
3. **Long-Term**: Cross-chain deployment, more privacy computing integrations, and standardized cooperation agreements with judicial/media/public welfare organizations, forming a verifiable global truth network.
